Gothura is the name of my font type. I chose this name because I wanted my font to be modern and to be under the category of a sans-serif font. But while going through the design process, an underlying gothic tone was created. In the font, the differentiations between the bold and thin lines give it a gothic look. What helped me with creating this font type was having a digital blueprint of my steps. In Illlustrator, I created my font the exact way I would in the future with coding. This way I could then see the shapes needed before starting my work.
